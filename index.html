About Me

Hi, I am Kishor Karki from Kathmandu, Nepal. I am a passionate designer and a front end web developer. I love what I do and I adore the creative process. It is addictive and can give never ending possibilities.

I graduated Bachelors in CSIT from Amrit Science Campus and I have knowledge and skills as a front end developer and a graphic designer. I use tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma and as well as HTML, CSS, JavaScript. I am excited to work alongside other amazing programmers and learn so much more!

Apart from my professional expertise, I like to play guitar, listen music and watch movies in my spare time. I enjoy going out to get fresh air to boost my creative mind. I am actively involved in numerous sites like FreeCodeCamp, Front End Mentor, CSS Battles, Codepen and much more for enhancing my CSS and Javascript skills . These sites, in my opinion are very useful for testing and improving your skills.
